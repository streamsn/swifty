FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before we get started.

How quickly can you start?
I can start on new projects within 24 hours of our agreement. If you have an urgent request, flag it in the contact form and I'll prioritize it.
What payment methods do you accept?
I accept PayPal, Venmo, and direct bank transfer. For larger projects, I typically ask for a 50% deposit upfront with the remainder on delivery.
What if I'm not happy with the work?
Your satisfaction is the goal. Every tier includes revisions so we can refine the work until it fully matches your vision — no extra charge.
Do you offer refunds?
If we realize early on that we aren't a good fit, I offer a full refund on any deposit. Once final deliverables are accepted, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.
Can I hire you for ongoing work?
Absolutely. The Premium tier is built for that. I also offer custom monthly retainer packages if you need consistent, long-term support.
How do I know you'll actually deliver?
I run on reputation and referrals. I keep communication clear and frequent so you're never left wondering about your project's status.
